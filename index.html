Why advocate?

Raise awareness

Illuminate silent crises — from air pollution to unequal access — and turn ignorance into public demand.

Influence & change policies

Policy is the invisible stethoscope: it touches millions. Advocacy rewrites those policies for justice.

Speak for the unheard

Represent individuals and communities who cannot speak for themselves. That’s the oath beyond the oath.

What is Advocacy?

Advocacy is the deliberate process to directly & indirectly influence decision‑makers, stakeholders and audiences — to support actions that fulfil human rights.

Advocacy Actions in practice

Awareness-raising Behaviour change Partnerships Lobbying Research/Publications Social mobilization Conferences & events Campaigning Policy-making

Craft messages that move mountains

The 5 C's of a key message

Clear Compelling Concise Consistent Convincing

Problem → Solution model: “We want [decision-maker] to do [action] because it will solve/improve [problem Z].”
Combine logic + emotion: facts protect the head, stories protect the heart. Example: tobacco control framed as protecting children — even when the goal is universal protection.

HEAD · HEART · HANDS

The three chambers of the advocate's soul

HEAD

Learn the policy, legislative landscape, partnerships and evidence. Knowledge without action is sterile — but action without knowledge is chaos.

HEART

Use your authentic voice and patient stories. Compel change by sharing the human cost. A single story can soften the hardest policy wall.

HANDS

Build coalitions, strengthen community power, and act collectively. No one changes a system alone — but together we rewrite laws.

Take home · The art of persuasion

Balance data & story

Facts and figures are anchors, but too many numbers sink the ship. Weave statistics with real-life narratives and photographs that sear into memory.

Effective spokesperson

Research deeply, speak with real-life stories, maintain a consistent visual style. Your credibility is your currency — invest it wisely.

Repetition × resonance

Key messages must be repeated across multiple channels, by multiple voices, until the truth becomes impossible to ignore.
